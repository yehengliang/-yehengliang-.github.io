Publications

  • Functional Random Forests for Curve Response

    Scientific Reports, Dec 17, 2021

    This publication introduces a novel functional random forests (FunFor) approach to model functional data response that is densely and regularly measured. The FunFor approach predicts curve responses for new observations and selects important variables from a large set of scalar predictors. It inherits the efficiency of traditional random forests in detecting complex relationships, including nonlinear and high-order interactions, without imposing parametric and distributional assumptions. The excellent performance of the FunFor approach is demonstrated in eight simulation settings and one real-data analysis. The FunFor approach has great potential for widespread application in various fields due to its minimal requirement on tuning parameters and its distribution-free and model-free nature. An R package named 'FunFor' implementing the FunFor approach is available at GitHub.
